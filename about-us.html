ABOUT US.

Gift Shop is our most enthusiastic debut project. Ever since we started to learn how to build a website with technologies HTML, CSS and Javascript, we have been excited and put our effort every single day into this project. We have been given an assignment to create a website which can provide our customers information of the products.

The website is designed in a manimalist style with sweet pastel colors of pink pallete, white and black. Which we hopefully can bring our customer a pleasant view and happiness when they visit the website.

We may put an end to their confusion about differences among products by creating a comparison function so that customers can see differences among other products. Not only that, we also allow our users to look up their favourite brand by filter with ease. Besides, if customer struggle with how to use the product, we create a page which they can find their product manual instruction and be able to download those documents to their computer.

This project was completed in nearly a month with the contribution of a group of 3 people who are dedicated, committed and hard-working.
